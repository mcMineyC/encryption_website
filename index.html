Message Encryption website

Usage tips:

Avoid leading and trailing spaces! They can affect the translation of your message. It is highly recommended to use underscores instead.

Pi Cipher

How it works: Add the digits of Pi to your letters! In the example message, "Hello World!", 3 will be added to H, making it K. 1 is added to e, making it f. Add 4 to l and get p, add 1 to l and get m, etc.

Stats:

  • Accepted characters: all
  • Case sensitive: yes
  • Underscores prefered: doesn't matter
  • Uses randomness in encryption: no
